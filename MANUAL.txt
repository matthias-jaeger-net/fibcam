CAM — USER MANUAL
=================


CAMERA MODE
-----------
The app opens in camera mode showing a live camera feed.

TOP BAR:
  Gyro toggle         (left)   — toggles gyroscope orientation guide
  Settings button     (middle) — toggles overlay saving in photos
  Switch camera       (right)  — toggles front/back camera

BOTTOM BAR:
  Shutter button (centre) — takes a photo

OVERLAYS:
  When overlays are enabled (via Settings), the following appear on the camera:
    • Rule of thirds grid lines
    • Gyroscope level indicator (when gyro is enabled)
    • Fibonacci squares and spirals (when in Fibonacci mode)

AFTER TAKING A PHOTO:
  An overlay shows the captured image.
  Download button (top left)  — saves the photo
  Close button    (top right) — returns to camera


FIBONACCI SQUARES MODE
----------------------
Press the Fibonacci button (top left corner) to enter Fibonacci mode.
The top bar changes to show Fibonacci controls.
The bottom-bar shutter button stays in the same position.

-- Placing the first square --
A guide square appears in the centre of the screen.
Tap anywhere to place the centre of the first square.
A message appears asking to "place the centre of the fibonacci squares".

-- Placing the second square --
Tap on any of the 4 sides of the first square to place the second
square. The direction you choose determines the orientation of the
spiral.

-- Adding more squares --
Each tap adds the next Fibonacci square. Squares grow following the
Fibonacci sequence (1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13 ...) and automatically
arrange into a spiral.

-- Controls (top bar during Fibonacci mode) --
  −         decrease the size of the active spiral
  +         increase the size of the active spiral
  ↻         rotate the spiral direction 90° (cycles through 4 directions)
  mirror    flip the spiral horizontally
  new       freeze the current spiral and start a new one
  delete    delete the active spiral
  fit       scale and centre the active spiral so it fills the screen

-- Spiral list (top left, appears when 2+ spirals exist) --
Spirals are labelled A, B, C ...
Tap a label to select and edit that spiral.
The active spiral is shown at full brightness; others are dimmed.
Deleting a spiral keeps the remaining labels unchanged
(e.g. deleting A from [A, B, C] leaves [B, C]).

-- Spiral arc --
After placing 3 or more squares, the Fibonacci spiral arc is drawn
inside the squares as a continuous quarter-circle curve.

-- Moving a spiral --
Tap and drag anywhere within the bounding box of the spiral to
reposition it.

-- Taking a photo --
Press the shutter button to capture the scene.
By default, spiral overlays are NOT included in the photo.
To include them, toggle the Settings button before taking the photo.
When overlays are enabled, the photo will include:
  • The Fibonacci squares and spirals
  • Rule of thirds grid lines
  • Any active gyroscope indicator
